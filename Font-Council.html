FONT COUNCIL

The courtroom of Typography
Overview

Welcome to the high-stakes world of elite design firms. In Font Council, you aren't just selecting a typeface; you are its legal defense team fighting for its typographic life. This is an intellectual battleground designed for four teams of sharp-eyed designers and persuasive debaters. The goal is simple but challenging: prove that your chosen font is the superior example of typographic anatomy against three rival firms in a grueling, 13-round courtroom battle.

The Concept: Typographic Litigation

The game transforms the abstract nuances of font design into concrete evidence in a trial setting. At the start of the game, each of the four teams pledges allegiance to one iconic font family (e.g., Open Sans, Helvetica, etc.). This font is your client. For the rest of the game, you must defend its honor, its curves, and its serifs against all criticism.

Colour Theory & Selection

The premium dark blue and shades of yellow in box and card designs elevate the legal theme.

Conclusion

A successful blend of design education and strategic gameplay, receiving positive feedback from design community.
